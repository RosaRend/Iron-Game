Game Play

×

The objective of the game is to time when your scrolling music note reaches the center of your equalizer. The closer you are the higher you score.

Layout

Each column is assigned one letter.

  • First Column: "a"
  • Second Column: "s"
  • Third Column: "d"
  • Forth Column: "f"

How to score:

  • Center wave: +100pts
  • Off center: +40pts
  • Off key: -25pts
